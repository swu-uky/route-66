The Main Street of America

Authored by: Shan Huan Wu

From 11/11/1926 to its decomission on 06/26/1985, Route 66 served as the main road for many migrating west. Stretching from Chicago, Illinois to Santa Monica, California, people today can still traverse this Main Street of America.

Data downloaded from ArcGIS Hub in September 2019, with country and state maps courtesy of Natural Earth.
